⚡ Warm-Up Protocol
▼
🚴
3–5 min General Primer Bike erg, row erg, or ski erg at easy Zone 2 pace. Just get blood moving — no effort required.
🔄
Dynamic Mobility (2–3 min) Leg swings × 10 each leg · Hip circles × 10 · Arm circles × 10 · Thoracic rotations × 8 · Inchworm × 5
🏋️
Exercise-Specific Ramp-Up (first heavy compound only) Do 2 warm-up sets before your first working set. Example for Squat: Bar × 10 → 50% × 6 → working weight. Everything after that — you're warm enough.
🦶
Leg Day Add-On Add 10 bodyweight squats + 10 glute bridges before lower-body sessions to activate the posterior chain.
⏱ Total warm-up should take 7–10 minutes max. Skip ramp-up on accessory exercises.
